Minshawis Hometown

He was born on October 30, 1943 in Al-Minsha’a, Sohag Governorate, southern Egypt. He memorized the Holy Qur’an at the age of eight. He is the son of the reciter Sheikh Siddiq Al-Minshawi, the brother of the famous reciter Muhammad Siddiq Al-Minshawi and the father of the reciter Sheikh Siddiq Mahmoud Al-Minshawi. He was able to join the Noble Qur’an Radio eight months after the death of his brother, the reader, Muhammad Siddiq Al-Minshawi.
